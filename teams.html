League Information

The Primera División, commonly known as La Liga, is the top professional football division of the Spanish football league system. Contested by 20 teams, it operates on a system of promotion and relegation with the Segunda División.

The Kazakhstan Premier League is the top division of football in Kazakhstan, organized by the Football Federation of Kazakhstan. Founded in 1992, it currently features 14 teams competing for the national championship.
